Hi! I am **Junteng Liu (刘俨腾)**, a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://github.com/HKUNLP), advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://jxhe.github.io/). I obtained my B.Eng. from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024, where I was also fortunate to work with Prof. Junxian He during my undergraduate studies.

I am broadly interested in **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**, with specific research focuses on:
- **LLM Reasoning & Reinforcement Learning** -- developing verifiable reasoning data and training methods for large language models.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** -- understanding and mitigating perception bottlenecks in multimodal models.
- **LLM Truthfulness & Interpretability** -- probing internal representations of LLMs for truthfulness and generalizability.
